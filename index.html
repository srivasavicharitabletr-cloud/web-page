Our Identity

Sri Vasavi Charitable Trust is more than just an organization; it is a promise of hope. We are a registered Public Charitable Trust driven by the spirit of selfless service and community upliftment.

Founded with the paramount intention of executing charitable and religious works for the betterment of society.
